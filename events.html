T-HACKS
SELL-IT-OFF

T-Hacks 4.0 is a 24-Hours virtual Hackathon where you will get the opportunity to interpret real-world problems by revamping them into something unusual. There are numerous themes to test your problem-solving skills. You can pick any theme and leverage technology to build solutions for real-life problems. Build the ideas you believe in and make a difference!

Top 3 winning Teams will get cash prizes and much more!
We have various perks for each participant. Check out the "Prizes" section on our website to know more.

Team Size: 1-5 Members

Last date to register: 14th October 2021

The competition is divided into two rounds:

First Round: Each participating team will be required to submit a product idea.
Final Round: Top 10 shortlisted teams will get the chance to pitch their product to a panel of judges. They will have to try to sell their product, and the most convincing team will win!

The top two winning teams will get Cash Prizes.
This first winning team will also get a company lifetime package from Clerky. It will help them build a company around the product idea.

Team Size: 1-4 Members

Last Date to Register: 12th October 2021
