On 22nd April 2021, IEEE PES ADGITM celebrated IEEE PES Day virtually to commemorate the changing of the name of Power Engineering Society to Power and Energy Society to focus on helping the environment and the society. Guest speakers for the event were Prof. Pierluigi Mancarella and Prof. Abdul Quaiyum Ansari.

Admission process in various universities, details of GRE, GMAT, IELTS, TOEFL were discussed in the event. 100+ students attended the event.
